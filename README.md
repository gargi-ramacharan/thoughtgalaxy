# thoughtgalaxy

a mind map of all your thoughts integrated into an AI agent. 

you need to vent about your teammates? thought galaxy.
you need to get a gift for your father for father's day? thought galaxy.
you need to remember the deadlines for all your assignments? thought galaxy.


thought galaxy can integrate reminders into your google calendar, can suggest you what to do if you have a question, or can give you emotional advice on how to approach things.
